Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Garlic
  • Canned tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Dried oregano or basil
  • Olive oil or butter
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions until it is tender but still slightly firm (al dente). Drain the water, but save a splash of it for later.
  2. Sauté the garlic: Heat olive oil or butter in a pan over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook for about 1 minute until it smells fragrant, being careful not to let it burn.
  3. Simmer the sauce: Pour the canned tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes into the pan with the garlic. Stir in the dried oregano or basil, salt, and pepper. Let it simmer gently for about 5 minutes to let the flavors blend.
  4. Combine everything: Toss the drained spaghetti directly into the pan with the sauce. If the sauce is too thick, add a splash of the saved pasta water to loosen it up.
  5. Mix and serve: Stir everything together until the noodles are completely coated, then plate it and serve hot.
