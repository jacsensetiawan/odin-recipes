Ingredients

  • Leftover cold rice
  • Garlic
  • Shallots or onion
  • Sweet soy sauce
  • Eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Fry the aromatics: Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Sauté the garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Scramble the egg: Push the aromatics to the side of the pan. Crack the egg into the empty space and scramble it until cooked.
  3. Add the rice: Turn the heat to high, add the cold rice, and break up any clumps with your spatula.
  4. Season: Pour in the kecap manis, add salt and pepper, and mix everything thoroughly until the rice is evenly brown and hot.
  5. Serve: Turn off the heat, plate your rice, and enjoy.
