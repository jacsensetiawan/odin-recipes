Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Nori sheets
  • Canned tuna mixed with mayo
  • Cucumber
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Prep the rice: Lay a sheet of nori (seaweed) shiny-side down on a flat surface or a bamboo rolling mat. Gently spread a thin, even layer of cooked sushi rice over the nori, leaving a 1-inch border empty at the top.
  2. Add the fillings: Place your fillings (like your tuna-mayo or cucumber strips) in a neat, straight line across the center of the rice.
  3. Roll it up: Lift the edge closest to you and roll it tightly over the fillings. Keep rolling forward, using gentle pressure, until you reach the empty border.
  4. Seal the roll: Wet the empty 1-inch border with a tiny bit of water using your finger. Finish rolling to seal the edge shut.
  5. Slice and serve: Use a very sharp knife wiped with a damp towel to slice the roll into bite-sized pieces. Serve with soy sauce.
